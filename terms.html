Goke Motor Services is a licensed car buying service that sources vehicles from dealer-only auctions on behalf of customers. We charge a flat service fee based on the vehicle price range, in addition to the wholesale auction price of the vehicle.

The service fee is paid upfront before we begin sourcing your vehicle. This fee covers auction access, vehicle inspection, paperwork handling, and dedicated support throughout the process.

We offer a 100% refund of the service fee under the following conditions:

The service fee is non-refundable once a vehicle has been purchased at auction with your approval. Once you approve a bid and the vehicle is won, the transaction is considered final.

6. Auction Warranty
